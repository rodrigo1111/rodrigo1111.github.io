Hi there!

When was the last time you struggled to make your customers understand your product?

How are you adapting your business to the new, online-only demands?

Are you actively listening to your customers needs and desires?

I am Fabio, a digital product designer deeply curious about the intersect between people, design and technology.

Over the years, I have partnered with ambitious companies across different market segments including governance, transportation, healthcare, finance, fashion and entertainment to deliver unique digital experiences with great user experiences that foster the connection between their business and their customers.

Today, I am working independently, giving each of my partners my full commitment. By focusing on active listening, working collaboratively and helping to create the right tools, I am improving the everyday lives of their customers through a design-led approach.

But, what is Prunast, you might ask…?

By finding and working with partners like you, I have the ambition to create a digital product design studio that focuses on helping companies to better understand their customers and apply a design-led approach to their products.

Initiatives like the Shaping Chaos podcast, enables our partners to learn from us and from the best around the world in managing creative teams, becoming great bosses and fostering great relationships.

Working Remotely

Great communication and web-based tools for design and prototype allow me to work with clients around the world. I am currently based in Porto, Portugal working from the Porto i/o Seaside, a co-working space where scientists, engineers, architects, planners share the space to meet, create and talk about their passions.

I am used to travelling to work and meeting my partners wherever they are based and I want you to feel free to hop on a plane and join me in this wonderful location anytime.
