dokspot enables medical device manufacturers to provide electronic instructions for use (eIFU) for their products and improve the way instructions are handled, updated and delivered.

We helped dokspot become a modern service provider by improving the overall platform experience to allow them to become a leader in health care solutions.

As dokspot started to scale, the overall platform experience started to deteriorate, making it harder to show prospective customers the platform's value and to retain their current customer base.

The brief was simple – to improve the overall user experience and allow customers to benefit from using the digital platform over the paperwork.

After months of collaboration, and through the thoughtful implementation of content strategy, user research, UX and UI in the platform, we built a robust experience that is helping dokspot to make digital instructions available for every medical manufacturer that wants to become more efficient and resourceful.

“Today, we are more confident in presenting and talking about our product.”

CYRILLE DERCHÉ - Founder & CTO at Dokspot

Accessibility by design

There are many touchpoints in device manufacturing. The process requires careful design and implementation of instructions for use that have to comply with a variety of global standards. Instruction writers and device manufactures have to work together to ensure there are no mistakes when an instruction goes live.

Initial research has shown that dokspot's validation process was convoluted and users were struggling to find the right action at the right time.

We've implemented a modern and easily scannable experience that unified the platform and allowed dokspot users to know what to do, at the right time.

A sacalable system

Dokspot client and server-side enables admin and clients to enjoy a consistent experience across the platform. We developed a library of components that can be used across multiple touchpoints and dokspot to scale their services without compromising on consistency. This system had to comply with the specific white-label solutions unique to dokspot's strategy.

Shared Ambition

Prunast empowered dokspot to move to the next stage of growth and be more confident when demoing the product to prospective clients. Bringing the value of design to industries with poor user experience like device manufacturing is allowing dokspot customers to stand out in the market.
